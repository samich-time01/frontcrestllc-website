Title: The Grand Duchy of Luxembourg: and the Treaty of London of 11 May 1867
Author(s): Emmanuel Servais, William McGovern
Description: The definitive firsthand account of the 1867 Treaty of London, by its co-author Emmanuel Servais, the 5th Prime Minister of Luxembourg. A seminal document in the nation's history, Servais reveals how rival empires resolved a continental crisis by guaranteeing the perpetual independence and neutrality of the world's only Grand Duchy.Newly translated into English by the editor.
