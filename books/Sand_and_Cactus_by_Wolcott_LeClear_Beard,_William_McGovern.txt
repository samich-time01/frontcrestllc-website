Title: Sand and Cactus
Author(s): Wolcott LeClear Beard, William McGovern
Description: The book "Sand and Cactus" by Wolcott Le Clear Beard is a collection of short stories set in the American Southwest, primarily Arizona and New Mexico. The stories revolve around various characters, including engineers, cowboys, gamblers, Mexicans, and Native Americans, highlighting the harsh realities and unique culture of the frontier.This edition contains a new introduction by the editor.
