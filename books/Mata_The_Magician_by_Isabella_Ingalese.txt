Title: Mata The Magician
Author(s): Isabella Ingalese
Description: Isabella Ingalese's "Mata the Magician" is a captivating romance of the new era that challenges societal norms and explores profound spiritual concepts. The story follows a young physician, Frank Bennet, whose life takes an extraordinary turn when he is unexpectedly drawn into the world of Mata, a mysterious young woman raised by her "Guru".
