Title: Attornal Punishment: Ten Ghost Stories Involving Lawyers
Author(s): William McGovern, Isabel G. Curtis, Mrs. Molesworth, Charlotte Riddell, Mary Raymond Shipman Andrews, Ellen Glasgow, Elinor Mordaunt, Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, Violet Hunt, Edith Wharton
Description: A lawyer's profession is to speak for the dead through wills and testaments. But in these ten chilling tales of spectral justice, the dead have found a way to speak back. The editor William McGovern, who is now in recovery from a spider bite, begins the anthology with an introduction of gothic proportion.
