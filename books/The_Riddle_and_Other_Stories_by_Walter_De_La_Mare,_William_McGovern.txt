Title: The Riddle and Other Stories
Author(s): Walter De La Mare, William McGovern
Description: A chilling short story collection, containing 15 of Walter De La Mare's greatest works: The Almond Tree, The Count's Courtship, The Looking Glass, Miss Duveen, Selina's Parable, Seaton's Aunt, The Bird of Travel, The Bowl, The Three Friends, Lispet, Lispett & Vaine, The Tree, Out of the Deep, The Creatures, The Riddle, and The Vats. This edition also features a new introduction by the editor.
