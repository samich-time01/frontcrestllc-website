Title: Caesar Cascabel
Author(s): Jules Verne, William McGovern, A. Estoclet
Description: A lively troupe of French showmen, the Cascabel family journey by wagon through the Americas and beyond, making friends whilst experiencing harsh realities of the frontier. The two part saga is filled with daring feats, and a persistent yet charming hero who will stop at nothing to achieve his goal.This edition includes a new introduction by the editor.
